Affiliate Creator Scheduler

Affiliate Creator Scheduler is a creator tool that helps TikTok creators manage affiliate videos, captions, hashtags, drafts, and posting workflow.

Features

  • Manage TikTok affiliate video drafts
  • Prepare captions and hashtags
  • Organize posting schedules
  • Connect TikTok accounts through OAuth
  • Support creators with content disclosure workflow

How It Works

Users can prepare their TikTok video content, manage captions and hashtags, organize publishing schedules, and connect their TikTok account through TikTok's official authorization flow.
